STEP 10: Client UI Polish
========================================================================

Goal: Update the match page to display the full card-based game state
with a polished experience.

  *** DESIGN DECISIONS NEEDED BEFORE STARTING ***

  D12. OPPONENT CARD VISIBILITY
       How much of the opponent's card state should be visible?
       - Option A: Full visibility (see all their items, tools, spells —
         allows counterplay and strategic decisions)
       - Option B: Hidden hand (see character/items/tools but not spells
         in hand — adds surprise element)
       - Option C: Minimal (only see character card and status effects —
         fog of war, more suspense)
       Recommendation: Option A for v1 — full visibility keeps the game
       strategic and transparent. Hidden info adds complexity.

  D13. CARD DETAIL VIEW
       When a player clicks/hovers a card, how much detail to show?
       - Option A: Tooltip on hover (name, description, stats)
       - Option B: Modal/popup with full card art and effect breakdown
       - Option C: Both (hover for quick info, click for full view)
       Recommendation: Option C — hover tooltip for quick glance, click
       for the full card view.

  D14. ANIMATION PRIORITY
       How much animation work for v1?
       - Option A: None (instant state updates, functional only)
       - Option B: Minimal (HP bar transitions, turn indicator pulse)
       - Option C: Full (damage numbers floating, card glow on trigger,
         status effect particles)
       Recommendation: Option B — CSS transitions on bars and a turn
       banner animation. Keep it lightweight for v1.

--- 10A: Player Info Panel ---

Show for each player:
  - Character card (name, element, image)
  - HP bar (current/max, colored by health %)
  - Energy bar (current/max)
  - Block shield icon with number
  - Status effect icons with remaining turn counters

--- 10B: Card Zone Display ---

  - Equipped items: row of small card tiles below character
  - Equipped tools: row of small card tiles
  - Hand: row of spell cards (clickable to play on your turn)
  - Graveyard: count indicator, expandable to see destroyed cards

--- 10C: Action Panel Redesign ---

Current: 3 buttons (Punch, Kick, Block)
New layout:
  - Basic attacks row: Punch, Kick, Block (always available)
  - Spell cards row: rendered from hand[], each with energy cost
  - Tool actions row: "Use [Tool Name]" for each equipped tool
  - Disable spells when energy < cost
  - Disable everything when not your turn

--- 10D: Turn Indicator ---

  - Animated "Your Turn" / "Opponent's Turn" banner
  - Turn number display
  - Timer (optional, for future turn time limits)
